Mindset Differences

  • Successful: Focus on solutions · Unsuccessful: Focus on problems
  • Successful: Long-term thinking · Unsuccessful: Instant gratification
  • Successful: Take responsibility · Unsuccessful: Blame others
  • Successful: View failure as feedback · Unsuccessful: View failure as defeat
  • Successful: Delay gratification · Unsuccessful: Seek pleasure now
